Restaurant to Another World


Synopsis

Nekoya is a restaurant that opens its doors to other worlds every “Saturhday”. It tells the story of the patrons that eat there, from humans to elves to animals that have human like physique and characteristics. The restaurant is run by a man referred to as Master and serves primarily Japanese and Western dishes.


Characters


Staff


Master

Master-He is the current owner of Nekoya, succeeding his grandfather. His parents died when he was young; so his grandparent took care of him.


Aletta

Aletta is a happy young demon. Her world makes it hard for her kind to find a job. She works as a waitress at Nekoya as her second job.


Kuro

Kuro is a dragon known as Black. She also works as a waitress, in her elf from, at Nekoya, to earn money, as suggested by her friend, Red.


Season 1 Customers


Red Queen

Red Queen is also known as Beef Stew. She is one of the dragons in the show. She is a close friend of Kuro.


Lionel

Lionel goes by Pork Cultlet Rice Bowl. He was captured and made to fight like a gladiator. Thanks to the wonderful food he ate, he won his battles.


Arius and Iris

Arius and Iris go by the alias, Carpaccio. They are a siren couple that settled on the island another patron used to live in.


Season 2 Customers


Romero and Julietta

Romero and Julietta go by Beef Steak. They are a vampiric couple that ran away from their village.


Pikke and Pakke

Pikke and Pakke go by the alias, Cream Croquette. They are a brother and sister that served delicious soup to the people in their world.


Artorius

Artorius is also known as Pork Loin Cutlet. He is one of the Four Heroes. He is great friends with Alexander.
